Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 29, 2026

iSOLD ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Data: When you register for an account, we collect information such as your name, email address, and business details. We use Firebase for secure authentication.

Business Data: Information related to your inventory, sales, customers, and financial transactions is stored securely in Google Firebase/Firestore.

Device Permissions:

  • Camera: Used solely for barcode and QR code scanning to manage inventory.
  • Location: Used for route planning and confirming sales representative visits.
  • Notifications: Used to send critical alerts regarding stock levels and task reminders.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data to provide, maintain, and improve our services, including processing transactions, providing customer support, and personalizing your user experience.

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures provided by Google Firebase to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

4. Advertising

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use information about your device to serve relevant ads. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for more details on how they handle data.

5. Third-Party Services

We use Google Services (Firebase, Firestore, AdMob) which have their own privacy policies regarding data usage.
